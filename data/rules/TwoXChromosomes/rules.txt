1. Respect: No hatred, bigotry, assholery, misogyny, misandry, transphobia, homophobia, racism or otherwise disrespectful commentary. Please follow reddiquette.
2. Equanimity: No drama-inducing crossposting of content found in other subreddits. Likewise, posts found to direct odious influxes here will be removed. Advertising your fabulous new subreddit is always welcome here.

You are welcome to link to relevant subreddits when warranted, but linking to comment threads is almost always considered drama-inducing regardless of intent. 

Relevant AMAs can be linked to. In subreddits smaller than 500k subscribers AMA's must be posted by a moderator of that subreddit.
3. Grace: No tactless posts generalizing gender. We are a welcoming community. Rights of all genders are supported here.
4. Relevance: We ask that you keep this community awesome by submitting content that is relevant to our experiences as women, for women, or about women. 

Several topics are not considered relevant, such as: Medical Advice; Relationship advice; Surveys, Research and Petitions; Horrible things said and done by individuals; Fundraising; Issues affecting men too; Questions for the perspectives of women; Posts made to debate the women of reddit; images outside image guidelines; "PM/chat me"; posts written by AI.
5. Post Etiquette: Post titles should not contain any “trigger” words and flair your post properly. 

Users should not be blindsided into words/phrases that affect them negatively. 

If you do post with either any of these words or phrases, your post will be removed and you’ll have to post it again with a revised title.
